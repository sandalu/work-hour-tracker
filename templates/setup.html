📅 When does your university semester start?

This is your academic start date — the first day of your semester. You won't be able to log hours before this date.

ℹ️ How fortnights work

The Australian government calculates your 48hr fortnightly limit in 14-day periods. Your fortnight starts on the Monday of your academic start week, and resets every 14 days after that.
